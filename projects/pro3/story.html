Our Story

Jacob and Emily’s story began in San Francisco, where their shared love for coffee sparked a deep connection. After years of exploring the city’s vibrant coffee culture, they moved to Harrisburg, Pennsylvania, and brought their dream to life by opening Gentle Grind, a cozy, community-centered coffee shop. Infused with warmth, art, and hospitality, Gentle Grind quickly became a local haven where every guest feels like family. Their journey is a testament to how love and passion can brew something truly extraordinary.
